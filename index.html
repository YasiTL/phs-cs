Computer Science Essentials & Principles

CSE and CSP are both year long introductory courses to Computer Science is aimed at all students who are intrested in CS. No Computer Science experience is needed. These classes cover various programming languages and highlight key principles of Computer Science.

AP Computer Science & Computer Science A

These college credit classes are aimed towards Junior and Senior students who have completed at least one introductory Computer Science course. You can expect to learn about programming with Java and Android App development.
